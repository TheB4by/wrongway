Frequently Asked Questions

How to use the tools?

To use the tools, download the .zip files from the provided links and extract them. Each tool comes with its own instructions for use. If you have difficulties, check the tutorials on the developers' websites.

How can I download the tools?

Simply click on the download links next to the tools and follow the instructions. Some tools come with installers for different operating systems.

Are the tools safe to use?

Yes, all tools provided are open-source and used by security professionals. However, always use them responsibly and legally. The author is not responsible for any misuse.

Can I use the tools on Linux?

Yes, many of the tools have versions for Linux. If installers are available for other operating systems, the links will also be on the download page.

Are my data registered on the website?

No, your data is not registered on the site. Only temporary information such as your IP is captured to enhance site experience and security. This data is encrypted and not stored long-term.
